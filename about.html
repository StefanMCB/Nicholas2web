Nicholas Fols, a prodigious Italian photographer, embodies the essence of fine art through his lens. With a unique blend of fashion photography and ethereal landscapes, his images possess a masterful, almost brushstroke-like quality. Each shot is a vivid statement to his imaginative vision and unyielding passion for capturing the beauty of the world around him.

Time seems to be a static unique lens, endless places that seem to exist, utopian stories and incredibly perfect narratives, and its mystery has made the photographer a lot of admirers.

Despite his young age, his name has gained international recognition in the fields of photography and fashion. In fact, Nicholas never shy away from putting himself in front of the camera except to create real masterpieces behind the lens.
